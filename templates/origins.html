Our Story

Born from bunker kitchens and fueled by post-war necessity, Vault Bites is more than a food chain — it’s a resistance.

In the smoldering ruins of 2287, when the surface became a wasteland and trust in fresh food was extinct, we emerged. We repurpose, reimagine, and resurrect forgotten ingredients from a broken world — making meals for survivors, by survivors.

Our Mission

In a world where good meals are rare, we believe food should be more than just rations. Our mission is to serve hearty, handcrafted meals inspired by the Fallout universe—whether you’re a scavenger, settler, or synth

Our Origin

Vault Bites was born not in kitchens of luxury, but in the shadows of fallout bunkers. In 2287, when the skies darkened and the earth cracked, hope was scarce — but hunger wasn’t.

Our first meals were crafted from salvaged supplies, nurtured by ingenuity. Survivors became chefs, and scraps became signatures. Today, every bite is a tribute to the resilience of those first days.

We don’t just serve food. We serve a story — of how we started, and why we never stopped.
